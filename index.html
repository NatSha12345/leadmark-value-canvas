MISTAKES · What’s really causing the pain LeadMark.AI

ROOT CAUSE

Buying AI tools instead of building AI knowledge. Any tool is only as good as the team using it — and most teams are never shown how.

WRONG THINKING

  • “I just need to find the right app”
  • “My IT person will figure it out”
  • “We’ll jump on it once things settle down”
  • “Our software vendor will add AI in the next update”

WRONG ACTION

  • Signed up for tools that nobody used after week two
  • Paid a developer to build something the team never touched
  • Did an AI workshop — everyone got excited, nothing changed
  • Sent one person on a course and hoped the knowledge would spread
